:: OUR JOURNEY

Building Pune's Skyline Since 2002

Balaji Construction started with a simple vision: deliver exceptional quality, integrity, and timely execution. Over two decades, we've transformed into a premier civil contracting firm known for residential townships, commercial complexes, luxury bungalows, and renovation projects across Pune and PCMC region.
